We, the Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants – CCC Chapter (JPIA – CCC Chapter), dedicate our optimum knowledge, talents, and intangible leadership skills to harness and fully utilize the skills of the Department of Business and Accountancy students by leading them with integrity, accountability, and passion. Furthermore, we implement activities that will significantly help them to become competitive professionals in the future.
The Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants – CCC Chapter (JPIA – CCC Chapter) envisions itself continuing the legacy of the former leaders of the organization and being the most active and best-performing organization and at the same time embodying the core values of City College of Calamba simultaneously. In addition, we aim to mark a tangible legacy by promoting an inclusive and harmonic environment for the students and equipping them with knowledge and experiences that will form part of their aspirations in their desired careers.
