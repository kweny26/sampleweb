This is the official website of the Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants - City College of Calamba

Nature of Organization: The JPIA-CCC Chapter is an Academic Organization that is dedicated for the Department of Business and Accountancy. It aims to help students to become competitive professionals in the future implementing activities related to both Bachelor of Science in Business and Accountancy (BSA) and Bachelor of Science in Accounting Information System (BSAIS) programs.
